About me

I am a triplet living in Brooklyn, NY studying Computer Information Systems at Baruch College. In 2015 I chose to challenge myself and relocate to New York on my own. With this change at such an early age, I not only learned a lot about myself as an individual but I had the opportunity to follow my passion for photography. If I had known my passion for photography would land my first job at an Apple retail store as a technician, I would have started earlier. While working in the Apple of World Trade Center, I quickly found my interest for leading teams, solving complex technical problems as well as the technology industry. I chose to step out of my comfort zone and experience other challenges, which encouraged me to apply to Google in 2017. With the achievement of passing the Google interviews, I knew that the technology industry was for me. After spending a 3 month internship in the Google NYC office, I was even more hungry for challenging projects. With that being said, I took the initiative to get my hands dirty by joining multiple startups with their work. To this day I have held a leadership role at Apple, two internships at Google and worked in a few self-starting startups to learn what it takes to become the best person that I can be.
